The Land of Giants

Posted on April 8th, 2021 at 12:00 PM

The 'Tierra de Gigantes' or Land of Giants is formally in Carolina, Puerto Rico. My old classmate Ramon would often tell tales about the place he was from. The origin of baseball Hall of Famer Roberto Clemente. A small place on a small island that jumped scale - nationally and internationally. Stories are told and embellished - they inspire us to make our own paths. Prometheus stealing fire from Mount Olympus - what is must have been like to see and FEEL the gods

On the intro to rapper Camron's debut album Confessions of Fire he faces a trial. Just in his mid 20's, he is already accomplished as a basketball player and a hustler, but he is new in the Hip Hop game. He knows his time to shine is coming but he is still apphensive - a fish out of water. His guide encourages him, 'Just Kick the Door in and Do What You Gotta Do'

We are faced with challenges of different orders in our lifetime. Things that were once important fade, but in the moment that are absolutely everything. As I embark on a new adventure and challenge I lean on the experience of knowing things will always work out - that experience is paramount - and I truly am the only one who can stop me
